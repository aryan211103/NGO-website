About Us

Ngo-needs is a platform that allows the user to donate commodities based on the need of the respective NGO’s. Donating to such NGOs ensures that resources are used effectively and go directly to those in need. A donation portal is a platform that allows user to donate commodities on the basis of the need of the respective NGO’s. Previously we were required to visit each NGO and ask them about their requirements, but now this problem can be easily solved by visiting a website which has a collection of NGO’s and their requirements. Also this is a huge platform for Donors as they have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on various causes, ranging from education and healthcare to environmental conservation and disaster relief
